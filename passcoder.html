The Passcoder

Try to find the correct passcode by using the 'clues' button. If you can figure out the
passcode, a trophy to do with the passcode will appear, and that is your reward.

See if you can discover all of the different passcodes and get all of the different trophies.

Always put your passcodes into the textbox underneath the title, and remember to listen to the clues you are given. Each trophy has it's own set of clues, and passcode. All trophies will appear below. If you enter in a passcode and you press submit but nothing happens, then it was the incorrect passcode. The question is, can you get all of the trophies?
