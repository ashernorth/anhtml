Asher North's HTML Index

Tap on a button to go to your desired website.

XYZ Worder is a very handy website that will give you a random noun, verb and adjective at the press of a button. You can also customise your theme, with different colour options, and dark mode.

The Passcoder is a game where you get certain clues, and for each set of clues, there is a trophy and a passcode. The clues will lead you to the correct passcode, and you can submit your answers when you think you have worked it out to hopefully unlock a trophy.

The Blasting Grounds is a game about blasting paper to unlock more and more effective blasters. Break unbelievable amounts of paper to complete the game.

Cane Toad Kerfuffle is a game about how a captain has sent out a message sixteen years ago for someone to deliver him as many cane toads as possible. You find the message and decide to do what he says.
