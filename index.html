XYZ Worder is a website that will give you a random noun, verb and adjective at the press of a button. You can also customise your theme, with different colour options, and dark mode.

The Passcoder is a game where you get certain clues, and for each set of clues, there is a trophy and a passcode. The clues lead you to the correct passcode, and you can submit your answers when you think you have worked it out.

The Blasting Grounds is a game where you can blast paper to unlock more and more effective blasters. Break unbelievable amounts of paper to complete the game.

Cane Toad Kerfuffle is a game where sixteen years ago a captain has sent out a message for someone to deliver him as many cane toads as possible, and he will tell you how to do so.
